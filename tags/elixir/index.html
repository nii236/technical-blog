Elixir June 16, 2016

All this Gophering around can get boring. That’s not a bad thing, I still consider Go the language with which I am strongest and most comfortable. If I had to use a language at my workplace, Go would be it. But sometimes you just want to poke around and see what else is out there. A language that offers a sense of adventure instead of the safety of Go and its static types, simplicity, rock solid linting and strict philosophy.
