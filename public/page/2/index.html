About

September 17, 2015

I’m an ex-control systems/automation engineer. I’ve been working in the mining industry for 6 years before switching to software development. During my engineering career I have spent about 3 years on mine sites, from Boddington to South Australia. I even spent nine months in Ghana, West Africa! A lot of my work involved switch configuration, PLC and SCADA programming, fault finding, code debugging, breakdown support and field testing. After my short career in engineering I started to have thoughts about finding work that lets me live close to civilisation.
